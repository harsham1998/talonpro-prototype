Other Placeholder Types

[link."type":"Concierge Link"|…]

Dynamic deep links to the guest portal (documents, surveys, check-in, etc.). Generated automatically by Zenya when sending.

[MANUAL]

Free-text field that the coordinator fills in manually before sending. Shown as an editable input in the send flow.

[date.today]

Automatically replaced with today's date when the message is sent.

[sender.mobile] · [user.creds]

Coordinator / sender information, filled from the logged-in user's profile.
